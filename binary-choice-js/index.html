Binary choice cockroach model


\(\frac{d X_i}{dt} = \mu _i\left(N - \sum_{j=1}^2 X_j\right) -\frac{\theta_i}{K^m+\left(\alpha_i X_i\right)^m}X_i \quad i=1,2\)
where
  • \(X_i\) is the mean number of sheltered individuals on shelter \(i\)
  • \(\mu_i\) is the individual probability to join shelter \(i\)
  • \(\theta_i\) is the individual probability to leave shelter \(i\)
  • \(\alpha_i\) is the inter-atraction in shelter \(i\)
  • \(K\) is a threshold value \(i\)
  • \(m\) is the cooperativity between individuals
